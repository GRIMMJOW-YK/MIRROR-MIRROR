About The Game

Mirror Mirror is not just a game - it's an experience that will challenge your perception of reality. Play carefully, as the reflections hold more power than you realize.

The Rules

1

Do Not Talk While Playing

Complete silence must be maintained during gameplay. Any vocalization may disrupt the delicate balance between dimensions.

2

Don't Move While Playing

Remain perfectly still once gameplay begins. Movement may attract unwanted attention from the other side.

3

Do Not Dare to Look Into the Mirror

Under no circumstances should you make direct eye contact with your reflection. What looks back may not be you.

WARNING: FAILURE TO FOLLOW THESE RULES MAY HAVE UNPREDICTABLE CONSEQUENCES
